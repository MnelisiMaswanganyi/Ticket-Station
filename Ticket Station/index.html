About Ticket Station

Get ready for a one-of-a-kind 3-day music festival in sunny South Africa that’s packed with experiences, social activities, multiple food stalls, bars, and so much more! Ticket Station is not just an escape from everyday pressures, it’s an opportunity to connect with diverse groups of people who share a common love of music and culture.

Parking & Camping


There is limited parking available at the festival. If you are going to drive in daily, please carpool in with your mates.
Camping at a festival is half the fun. We have various options for you to choose from.

Food & Drinks

We know that everyone’s tastes are different, and we got you! The festival will boast multiple food stalls and bars… with so many options available, you will have something new to try every day. The festival is cashless so make sure you pre-load your accreditation with your moola to make sure your buying experience at the festival is uninterrupted.
